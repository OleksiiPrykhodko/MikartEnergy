Mikart-Energy Story


Founded in 2021


Railsware was established in 2007 by four tech-savvy enthusiasts. At that time, we mainly focused on clients’ projects providing great code and delivering features our customers requested. Even though we had little experience in product management back then, we always wanted to participate in all stages of a product life cycle.


Today Railsware is a Project company with two main focuses – services and products. These directions are integrally connected and complement each other. For us, it doesn’t matter what type of project we work on. We apply the same approaches and tools to all of them to accumulate and share knowledge and improve our craft with each new case.
