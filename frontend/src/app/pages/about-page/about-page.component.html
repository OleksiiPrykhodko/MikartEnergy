Mikart-Energy Story


Founded in 2021


Mikart Energy was founded in 2021 by two tech-savvy enthusiasts. At that time, we mainly worked on customer projects, creating great solutions and providing features that our customers demanded. Even though we didn't have much experience with the full project cycle back then, we always wanted to be involved in all phases of the product lifecycle.


Today Mikart Energy is a engineering company with two main areas - services and sales. These areas are inextricably linked and complement each other. It does not matter for us what type of project we are working on. We apply the same approaches and tools to all of them in order to accumulate and share knowledge and improve our craft with each new project.
